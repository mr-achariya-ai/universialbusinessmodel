Our Story

From idea to global network

2022

The Idea

AALAI was founded after our founders kept noticing a painful gap: talented professionals and great businesses couldn't find each other efficiently. Traditional networks felt outdated and noisy. We knew AI could do better.

2023

Building the Core

We launched our private beta with 500 handpicked members and began training our AI matching engine. Early feedback validated that smart recommendations were 4x more likely to lead to real business outcomes.

2024

Public Launch

AALAI opened to the public and grew to 50,000+ members within the first year. We introduced the Opportunity Board, Business Profiles, and Network Analytics dashboard.

2025

Going Global

Expanded to 120+ countries with multilingual support and regional business networks. Partnerships with 12,000+ businesses worldwide helped us scale impact across every industry.

2026

Today & Beyond

We're continuously evolving — launching deeper AI tools, industry-specific networks, and a Universal Business Model framework that gives every member a structured path to growth.
